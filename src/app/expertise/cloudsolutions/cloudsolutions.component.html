Kiosk & Touchscreen

We provide custom kiosk software development for our customer’s current and future needs. Our kiosk & touchscreen development team builds efficient UIs, multi-tenant services, hardware interfaces, branding management, resource management, and remote administration. We offer a range of kiosk software with many hardware interfacing for various business. We have significant experience in designing and developing interactive kiosks software for a wide range of industries such as smart cities, parking control systems, hospitality services, and casino gaming systems.

Web Applications

We build custom tailored web solutions to fit our customer’s business goals. Our team work backwards from customer requirements and focuses on user experience (UX), high performance, availability, and scalability. We always use the most apt technology while staying focused on the business requirements. We provide UX (mockups/wireframes) for all customer journey, UI design, application development considering security and data privacy, quality assurance, and operations support. Desktop Applications

At Addant, we help our customers with their unique need of custom desktop applications targeting two operating systems – Windows and macOS. Our development team has built extensible desktop applications leveraging the best architecture and infrastructure. The team at Addant has the technical expertise and extensive experience to solve our clients’ complex issues. We pay proper attention to business while choosing the best fit UI technology. We also understand that theming is important for such applications, and we validate it in design phase. Deployment strategy is also very important, we consider various technology options for optimal customer experience.

Mobile Applications

Be it consumer or enterprise based, Addant offers design, development, deployment, and maintenance of value-added mobile applications across iOS & Android platforms. Our team has significant experience when it comes to native or cross-platform development, and they focus on creating intuitive interfaces ensuring seamless user engagement. We always partner with our customers in understanding the features they need and want. Then we sketch the wireframes to describe the customer journey before starting the full cycle development.

Cloud Solutions

We recognize the paradigm shift from the traditional software development to the modern applications development, leveraging cloud tools. Cloud applications bring higher scalability, easier management, and reduced cost through microservices, better automation and DevOps practices. It is our responsibility to help our customers effectively meet the demands of their users and remain competitive. At Addant, we provide cloud development and support for our customers leveraging Amazon AWS and Microsoft Azure stack.
